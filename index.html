Helping Youth Reach Their Potential

North Star RTC is a newly established Residential Treatment Center providing 24-hour supervision and therapeutic services to youth ages 6 to 12 years old with emotional and behavioral disorders in DFPS care. Our highly trained professional staff use evidence-based programs that focus on the wholistic development of each child with therapeutic services to meet their individual needs.


****North Star RTC works with DFPs and Community Based Care Contracts.  We do not accept private pay or insurance at this time.
